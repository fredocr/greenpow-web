# Marketing Content Rules

Use these rules whenever generating GREENPOW website, campaign, sales, SEO, editorial, or landing-page copy.

1. Position GREENPOW as a carbon-aware cloud company, not just a hosting company.
2. Adapt messaging by tier: T1, T2, or T3.
3. Adapt tone by region: Europe or LATAM.
4. Avoid greenwashing and unsupported environmental claims.
5. Prefer clear, practical, evidence-oriented language.
6. Mention privacy, security, and operational efficiency alongside sustainability.
7. Use "carbon-aware cloud" consistently.
8. Use local language for LATAM campaigns.
9. Use compliance and ESG language for EU enterprise content.
10. Create CTAs that match buyer maturity.
11. Avoid overpromising certifications, emissions reductions, or compliance.
12. Where proof is missing, mark the claim as needing verified evidence before publication.
13. For technical pages, layer complexity: simple overview first, details later.
14. For enterprise pages, lead with risk reduction, traceability, and support.
15. For startup pages, lead with affordability, simplicity, and mission.
16. For scaleup pages, lead with performance, ESG visibility, and growth.
17. Always connect sustainability to a business outcome.
18. Keep copy short and modular for web components.
19. Never use generic eco fluff.
20. Never claim GREENPOW is carbon neutral unless verified by current proof.

Before publishing, check `context/claims/claims-policy.en.md`.
