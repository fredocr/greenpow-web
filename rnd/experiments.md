## Initial Experiment Ideas

Name: ESG landing page message test
Hypothesis: ESG and CSRD buyers respond to infrastructure-level Scope 2 visibility.
Audience: Sustainability and technology leaders.
Method: Compare demo requests from ESG-focused landing copy against generic sustainable hosting copy.
Success metric: Higher qualified demo conversion rate.
Decision after experiment: Promote winning message into `context/sales/` and relevant vertical pages.

Name: AI infrastructure message test
Hypothesis: AI teams respond to carbon-aware scheduling when framed around workload flexibility and cost discipline.
Audience: AI operations and automation teams.
Method: Test AI landing page CTA language and demo request themes.
Success metric: More qualified AI infrastructure conversations.
Decision after experiment: Update `context/verticals/ai.*.md` and `context/products/products.*.md`.
