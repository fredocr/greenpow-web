# Hypotheses

Use this file for testable assumptions about GREENPOW's market, product, positioning, and website.

## Current Hypotheses

1. Companies with ESG and CSRD pressure will respond better to infrastructure-level carbon visibility than generic sustainability messaging.
2. AI workload growth makes carbon-aware compute scheduling more commercially relevant.
3. GREENPOW should lead with product-led infrastructure technology to avoid being perceived only as hosting or managed services.
4. MySx can become a defensible optimization layer if it combines carbon intensity, energy price, cost, performance, and infrastructure availability.
5. Cortex can clarify GREENPOW's AI-agent story by giving autonomous workflows a static organizational memory layer.
