# GREENPOW RND Bucket

This folder is for research, hypotheses, experiments, and future product ideas.

RND content can inform product, website, sales, and investor work, but it is not approved source-of-truth content until promoted into:

- `context/`
- `PROJECT_BRIEF.md`
- `docs/decisions/`

## Good Uses

- Product hypotheses.
- MySx research.
- Cortex research.
- Carbon data source notes.
- Competitor analysis.
- Prototype and experiment notes.
- Future roadmap ideas.

## Do Not Use RND For

- Final website copy.
- Legal claims.
- Production metadata.
- Approved pricing.
- Confirmed product commitments.
- Public impact claims.

## Promotion Rule

When RND becomes approved, record the decision in `docs/decisions/` and move the usable content into the relevant `context/` file.
