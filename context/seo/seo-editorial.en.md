## Content Format Rules

### T1 Content

Best formats: short posts, launch guides, quick tutorials, deployment guides, social proof, community content, and GitHub examples.

Ideal length: 300-900 words.

CTA examples: Start building. Try GREENPOW. Deploy now.

### T2 Content

Best formats: case studies, webinars, technical explainers, ROI pages, migration guides, comparison pages, and sustainability reporting guides.

Ideal length: 900-2,000 words.

CTA examples: Book a demo. See ESG dashboard. Talk to solutions team.

### T3 Content

Best formats: executive briefs, white papers, procurement documentation, RFP templates, compliance pages, technical architecture docs, and ESG tools.

Ideal length: 1,500-4,000 words.

CTA examples: Request enterprise briefing. Download RFP pack. Contact GREENPOW.
