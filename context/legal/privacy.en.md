# Privacy Policy

GREENPOW collects personal data submitted through website forms, including name, email, company, role, country, interest type, message, preferred language, source page, and campaign parameters when available.

This data is used to respond to requests, manage commercial conversations, support partner and investor inquiries, provide customer support, and improve website operations.

Form submissions may be processed through Pipedrive as GREENPOW's customer relationship management system.

GREENPOW does not sell personal data. Data may be shared with service providers that support website hosting, analytics, CRM operations, email communication, and security.

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their personal data by contacting GREENPOW through the website contact form.

This page should be reviewed by legal counsel before publication.
