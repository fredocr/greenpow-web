# Impact, LCA, And MySx

## Impact Narrative

GREENPOW reduces the carbon footprint of cloud computing by making compute placement more responsive to carbon intensity, energy price, infrastructure availability, cost, and performance.

## Why Static Cloud Decisions Are A Problem

Cloud architectures often choose regions, providers, or hosting environments during setup and then keep those decisions fixed. Energy markets, grid carbon intensity, cost, and infrastructure availability change constantly.

GREENPOW turns these changing signals into infrastructure decisions.

## MySx

MySx is GREENPOW's algorithmic layer for deciding where and when workloads should run.

It should be explained as an optimization layer that can consider:

- Carbon intensity.
- Energy price.
- Infrastructure availability.
- Cloud or hosting cost.
- Performance requirements.
- Workload urgency.
- Compliance constraints.

## Impact Levers

- Scope 2 visibility.
- Carbon-aware region and zone selection.
- Load shifting.
- Workload scheduling.
- Auto-offsetting as a complementary mechanism.
- LCA-informed infrastructure decisions.

## Claim Discipline

Do not claim zero emissions or guaranteed carbon reduction for every workload. Use precise language: reduce, optimize, improve visibility, support lower-carbon decisions, and enable carbon-aware scheduling.
