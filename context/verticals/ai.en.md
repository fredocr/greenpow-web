# Use Case: AI

## Hero

Carbon-aware infrastructure for AI workloads and autonomous organizations.

## Problem

AI increases compute demand, scheduling complexity, and scrutiny over energy consumption.

## Why Now

Organizations are deploying AI workflows faster than their infrastructure governance can adapt.

## GREENPOW Solution

GREENPOW helps place and schedule AI-related workloads using carbon, energy, cost, performance, and availability signals.

## Relevant Product Modules

- AI Agents Infrastructure.
- Public Cloud Optimization.
- Cortex.
- MySx.

## Impact Angle

Support lower-carbon AI compute decisions, especially for flexible or scheduled workloads.

## Business Benefit

Improve infrastructure discipline for AI operations while giving sustainability teams clearer signals.

## Example

An AI operations team schedules non-urgent processing during lower-carbon windows and uses Cortex to provide agents with stable organizational context.

## FAQ

### Is GREENPOW only for AI workloads?

No. AI is one important use case, but GREENPOW also supports cloud, hosting, private cloud, and distributed infrastructure optimization.
