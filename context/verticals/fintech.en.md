# Use Case: Fintech

## Pain

Fintech companies need security, performance, compliance, and trust.

## Message

GREENPOW gives fintech teams secure, privacy-first infrastructure with measurable sustainability advantages.

## Content Angle

Secure cloud infrastructure for fintech growth.

## CTA

Build compliant cloud foundations.

## Relevant Product Modules

- Cloud-as-a-Service.
- Private Cloud.
- Public Cloud Optimization.
- GreenOps for AI where compute-heavy workloads are involved.

## Buyer Emphasis

Lead with security, privacy-first infrastructure, traceability, and operational resilience. Sustainability should strengthen the trust story, not distract from compliance and reliability.
