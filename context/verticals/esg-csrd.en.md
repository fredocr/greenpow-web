# Use Case: ESG And CSRD

## Hero

Carbon-aware cloud infrastructure for ESG and CSRD reporting pressure.

## Problem

Organizations need better visibility into the emissions impact of digital infrastructure, but cloud and hosting decisions are often made without granular carbon signals.

## Why Now

CSRD and ESG expectations increase pressure to connect infrastructure choices with measurable reporting and reduction pathways.

## GREENPOW Solution

GREENPOW helps teams compare and route workloads using carbon, energy, cost, and infrastructure signals.

## Relevant Product Modules

- Public Cloud Optimization.
- Private Cloud.
- VPS & Web Hosting.
- MySx.

## Impact Angle

Scope 2 visibility, lower-carbon placement decisions, load shifting, and LCA-informed infrastructure strategy.

## Business Benefit

Give sustainability and technology teams shared infrastructure data for reporting, reduction planning, and operational decisions.

## Example

A company uses GREENPOW to identify when and where non-urgent workloads can run with lower carbon intensity while preserving business requirements.
