# Use Case: Blockchain

## Pain

Blockchain workloads face scrutiny around energy consumption, reliability, and credibility.

## Message

GREENPOW helps blockchain teams improve infrastructure efficiency and tell a stronger sustainability story.

## Content Angle

Lower-carbon infrastructure for distributed technologies.

## CTA

Deploy sustainable blockchain infrastructure.

## Relevant Product Modules

- Public Cloud Optimization.
- Private Cloud.
- GreenOps for AI and compute-intensive workloads.
- Carbon-aware workload scheduling.

## Buyer Emphasis

Lead with infrastructure efficiency, credibility, carbon visibility, and reliability. Avoid implying that GREENPOW changes the emissions profile of blockchain protocols unless supported by workload-specific proof.
