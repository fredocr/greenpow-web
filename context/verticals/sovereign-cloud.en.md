# Use Case: Sovereign Cloud

## Hero

Carbon-aware infrastructure for sovereign and hybrid cloud needs.

## Problem

Organizations need more control over data location, infrastructure governance, and operational resilience while still managing cloud cost and emissions.

## Why Now

Sovereignty, compliance, and resilience are becoming board-level cloud concerns.

## GREENPOW Solution

GREENPOW supports private, hybrid, and regional infrastructure strategies with carbon-aware optimization logic.

## Relevant Product Modules

- Private Cloud.
- Public Cloud Optimization.
- MySx.
- VPS & Web Hosting.

## Impact Angle

Combine governance and control with visibility into energy, carbon, and infrastructure placement.

## Business Benefit

Support data-residency and control requirements without ignoring sustainability and cost.

## Example

A regulated organization keeps sensitive workloads on controlled infrastructure while optimizing flexible workloads across approved lower-carbon environments.

## FAQ

### Does sovereign cloud mean avoiding public cloud entirely?

No. A sovereign strategy can include private, hybrid, and approved public cloud infrastructure depending on the organization's requirements.
