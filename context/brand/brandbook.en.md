# GREENPOW Brandbook

## One-Line Positioning

GREENPOW is a carbon-aware cloud infrastructure company reducing the carbon footprint of cloud computing by optimizing where and when compute runs.

GREENPOW is also described as a carbon-aware cloud platform that helps businesses run secure, scalable digital infrastructure with lower emissions and measurable sustainability impact.

## Mission

Reduce the climate impact of cloud computing while keeping infrastructure useful, scalable, and economically viable for modern organizations.

Advance technology by optimizing renewable energy usage and providing secure, efficient, scalable cloud infrastructure that helps organizations reduce environmental impact while improving digital operations.

## Vision

Lead the global transition toward carbon-aware cloud computing and redefine how digital infrastructure supports a sustainable, interconnected future.

## Brand Category

Primary category: carbon-aware cloud computing.

Secondary categories:

- Low-emission cloud infrastructure.
- Green private cloud.
- Sustainable cloud operations.
- Carbon-aware workload optimization.
- Privacy-first cloud infrastructure.
- ESG-ready cloud services.

## Positioning Statement

GREENPOW is the carbon-aware cloud platform for organizations that need secure, scalable, privacy-first infrastructure with measurable environmental impact.

Unlike traditional cloud providers that optimize mainly for performance, cost, or scale, GREENPOW optimizes for performance, cost, security, and carbon awareness together.

## Brand Promise

Cleaner compute without forcing teams to give up cloud flexibility, performance, or operational control.

GREENPOW helps organizations reduce the environmental impact of cloud infrastructure while maintaining performance, security, and control.

Supporting promises:

- Run cloud workloads with lower carbon impact.
- Understand infrastructure-related emissions.
- Improve sustainability reporting.
- Protect data with privacy-first infrastructure.
- Reduce cloud complexity.
- Deploy private cloud and managed cloud solutions.
- Scale infrastructure with sustainability built in.
- Align technology operations with ESG goals.
- Prepare for future compliance and carbon reporting expectations.

## Audience

- Technology leaders managing public cloud, private cloud, VPS, or hosting infrastructure.
- Companies with ESG, CSRD, carbon accounting, or Scope 2 reduction goals.
- AI-native organizations and autonomous-agent teams.
- Investors focused on infrastructure, climate tech, AI, and sovereign cloud.
- Partners with cloud, data center, hosting, MSP, or sustainability distribution channels.

## Personality

Precise, credible, pragmatic, technical, and commercially focused.

GREENPOW should sound like infrastructure technology, not a lifestyle sustainability brand. Avoid vague green claims. Lead with operational signals, measurable reduction paths, and infrastructure decision-making.

## Core Messages

- Cloud decisions are often static, but energy, carbon, cost, and performance change continuously.
- GREENPOW helps organizations route and operate workloads using real-time carbon, energy, cost, and infrastructure signals.
- The platform supports public cloud, private cloud, VPS, hosting, and distributed infrastructure.
- MySx is the algorithmic layer that helps decide where and when compute should run.
- Cortex gives autonomous agents a structured memory layer for organizational context, process knowledge, and decision support.

## Strategic Narrative

Digital infrastructure is growing rapidly. AI, SaaS, blockchain, fintech, e-commerce, and data-heavy businesses are increasing demand for compute power, storage, and always-on infrastructure.

This growth creates five major problems:

1. Cloud energy demand is increasing.
2. Companies need better visibility into their digital carbon footprint.
3. Sustainability teams need credible data for ESG and Scope 2 or Scope 3 reporting.
4. CTOs and CIOs need secure, reliable, cost-efficient infrastructure.
5. Enterprises and governments need privacy, compliance, traceability, and regional control.

Traditional cloud providers rarely give customers simple, actionable tools to understand and reduce the carbon impact of their infrastructure.

GREENPOW answers this with carbon-aware cloud infrastructure that combines renewable-energy-aware workload logic, low-emission infrastructure, private and managed cloud services, security and privacy by design, carbon metrics and traceability, operational simplicity, and regional delivery and support.

## Core Belief

The future of cloud computing will not be judged only by speed, uptime, and cost. It will also be judged by carbon performance, data sovereignty, operational transparency, compliance readiness, regional resilience, and trust.

Cloud infrastructure should be aware of the energy behind it.

## Preferred Terms

- Cloud infrastructure optimization
- Workload routing
- Carbon intensity
- Energy price signals
- Infrastructure availability

- Load shifting

- Sovereign and hybrid cloud

## Terms To Avoid

- Greenwashing language
- Generic eco hosting
- Only managed hosting
- Charity offsetting as the primary value proposition
- Claims of zero impact unless legally and technically substantiated

## Brand Voice

GREENPOW should sound:

- Clear.
- Credible.
- Practical.
- Technical but understandable.
- Trustworthy.
- Sustainability-driven.
- Regionally aware.
- Optimistic but not naive.
- Confident but not exaggerated.

Avoid sounding hype-driven, vague, overly academic, greenwashing-heavy, too corporate, too casual for enterprise buyers, or too technical for general pages.

## Writing Principles

1. Lead with business value, then explain sustainability value.
2. Use "carbon-aware" consistently.
3. Avoid vague sustainability claims.
4. Write in plain language.
5. Make every claim measurable or explainable.
6. Adapt message by tier and region.
7. Use CTAs that match buyer readiness.
8. Keep technical detail available but layered.
9. Prioritize trust over hype.
10. Always connect sustainability to performance, risk, or compliance.
