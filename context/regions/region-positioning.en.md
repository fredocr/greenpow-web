# Region-Specific Positioning

## Europe

### Primary Language

English-first, with local variants for DACH, Nordics, Spain, France, and other European markets.

### Tone

- Rational.
- Precise.
- Compliance-aware.
- ESG-oriented.
- Technical but clear.
- Trustworthy.
- Transparent.

### Audience Psychology

- T1: Idealistic. Lead with mission and ethical cloud.
- T2: Rational. Lead with ROI, ESG metrics, and scalability.
- T3: Compliance-driven. Lead with traceability, sovereignty, and audit readiness.

### Tier Positioning

- T1 EU: Agile, ethical, transparent cloud.
- T2 EU: Mature, scalable platform with ESG metrics.
- T3 EU: CSRD-aligned, ESG-driven transformation enabler.

### Preferred Proof Points

- Carbon metrics.
- Renewable-energy awareness.
- Data protection.
- Sovereign and private cloud options.
- ESG reporting support.
- Technical transparency.
- Security architecture.
- Uptime and reliability.
- Open standards.

## LATAM

### Primary Language

Spanish-first and Portuguese-first depending on market.

### Tone

- Trust-building.
- Practical.
- Regionally aware.
- Human.
- Accessible.
- Credible.
- Growth-oriented.
- Cost-conscious.

### Audience Psychology

- T1: Resilient. Lead with affordability and global-grade access.
- T2: Ambitious. Position GREENPOW as infrastructure for growth.
- T3: Risk-averse. Lead with credibility, local support, and compliance.

### Tier Positioning

- T1 LATAM: Affordable, global-grade, carbon-smart starter stack.
- T2 LATAM: Cost-efficient scaling with future-ready compliance.
- T3 LATAM: Certified, locally supported, risk-mitigated infrastructure.

### Preferred Proof Points

- Local support.
- Spanish and Portuguese communication.
- Cost efficiency.
- Global-grade infrastructure.
- Reduced operational complexity.
- Green differentiation.
- Trust and credibility.
- Practical compliance readiness.
- Regional case studies.
- Clear onboarding.

## Language Guidelines

### English

Use for EU default pages, global pages, technical docs, investor-facing material, and enterprise material.

Use international English unless a specific market requires a local variant.

### Spanish

Use for LATAM campaigns, LATAM landing pages, regional sales material, and trust-building communications.

Example tagline: Infraestructura cloud segura, eficiente y consciente del carbono.

### Portuguese

Use for Brazil-specific campaigns, Sao Paulo ecosystem content, and LATAM enterprise or scaleup expansion.

Example tagline: Infraestrutura cloud segura, eficiente e consciente de carbono.
