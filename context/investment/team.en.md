## Investor Decision Criteria To Address

The website must proactively answer the questions investors will ask:

1. Team quality: can this team execute infrastructure and enterprise GTM?
2. Market size: is this a venture-scale opportunity?
3. Timing: why now?
4. Traction: is there real customer demand?
5. Unit economics: can this scale without burning too much capital?
6. Defensibility: why can't AWS, Google, Azure, OVH, or Hetzner copy this easily?
7. Climate impact: is the carbon reduction measurable and material?
8. Regulatory tailwinds: does GREENPOW benefit from CSRD, AI Act, ESG, Scope 2, and sovereignty pressure?
9. GTM clarity: who buys first, why, and through what channel?
10. Series A path: what milestones will this round unlock?
