# Investor FAQ

## What Is GREENPOW?

GREENPOW is a compute infrastructure platform and control layer that helps customers run workloads where cost, energy, latency, and compliance conditions are most favorable.

## Is GREENPOW A Cloud Provider?

GREENPOW provides distributed compute and private cloud as a service, but its strategic value is the control layer that optimizes workload placement across infrastructure.

## What Makes GREENPOW Different From AWS, Azure, Or Google Cloud?

Hyperscalers mostly provide carbon reporting and customer-side sustainability dashboards. GREENPOW uses energy and carbon signals as operational inputs for workload placement, especially across private, hybrid, and distributed infrastructure.

## What Is MAIZX?

MAIZX is GREENPOW's proprietary workload-placement and optimization framework. It ranks infrastructure options based on cost, carbon intensity, energy efficiency, latency, compliance, and workload requirements.

## What Is Carbon Ledger?

Carbon Ledger is GREENPOW's emissions and energy tracking layer. It supports granular Scope 2 visibility and enables carbon-aware optimization.

## Who Buys GREENPOW?

Initial customers include developers, AI agencies, web/API businesses, and companies looking for predictable compute. Expansion customers include regulated enterprises requiring private cloud, sovereignty, compliance, ESG reporting, and managed infrastructure.

## How Does GREENPOW Make Money?

GREENPOW monetizes through usage-based distributed compute and recurring private-cloud-as-a-service contracts. The long-term revenue mix is expected to shift toward larger enterprise/private cloud contracts.

## What Is The Current Traction?

Current investor materials show approx. USD 500K ARR, approx. 300 active subscribers, 1,300+ services running, and approx. 78% gross margin.

## What Is The Current Round?

GREENPOW is raising approx. EUR 3M-EUR 3.5M Seed at approx. EUR 12M pre-money valuation, with approx. 20%-24% equity offered, through priced equity and/or convertible loans.

## What Does The Seed Round Unlock?

The round supports infrastructure expansion, commercial growth, MAIZX and Carbon Ledger development, compliance readiness, and an 18-24 month path to Series A readiness.
