# Sales And Advertising Context

## Sales Positioning

GREENPOW helps organizations reduce the climate impact of cloud infrastructure while improving visibility into carbon, cost, and operational placement decisions.

## Primary Sales Angles

- Reduce cloud emissions with carbon-aware workload routing.
- Improve Scope 2 visibility for cloud and hosting infrastructure.
- Optimize compute placement using real-time carbon, energy, cost, and infrastructure signals.
- Modernize hosting, VPS, and cloud infrastructure.
- Prepare infrastructure for AI-enabled workflows and autonomous operations.

## Buyer Pain Points

- Cloud decisions are static while carbon, cost, and energy conditions change.
- Sustainability teams lack infrastructure-level signals.
- Engineering teams need practical tools, not abstract ESG claims.
- Companies want lower-carbon infrastructure without sacrificing reliability.
- AI workloads increase compute demand and scrutiny.

## Conversion Strategy

### T1 Funnel

Awareness: social, Dev.to, YouTube, GitHub, and founder communities.

Consideration: landing pages, pricing, quick-start guides, and community proof.

Conversion: free trial, self-service signup, low-cost VPS, or starter stack.

Retention: usage tips, sustainability badges, and community updates.

### T2 Funnel

Awareness: webinars, SEO, LinkedIn, vertical campaigns, and partner content.

Consideration: case studies, demos, technical proof, and ESG dashboard preview.

Conversion: live demo, migration assessment, or pilot project.

Retention: quarterly optimization reviews, sustainability reports, and upsell to managed or private cloud.

### T3 Funnel

Awareness: executive content, events, institutional partnerships, and ESG reports.

Consideration: white papers, architecture review, security review, and procurement pack.

Conversion: private consultation, pilot, RFP process, and white-glove onboarding.

Retention: SLA reviews, audit support, ESG reporting, and strategic roadmap sessions.

## Ad Messaging Guardrails

Use precise claims. Avoid promising guaranteed emissions cuts without workload context. Prefer "reduce", "optimize", "route", "measure", "improve visibility", and "support lower-carbon decisions".

## Content Quality Checklist

Before publishing any GREENPOW page, verify:

- The page clearly says what GREENPOW does.
- It uses "carbon-aware cloud" correctly.
- The target audience is clear.
- The region is clear when region matters.
- The CTA fits the buyer stage.
- Environmental claims are specific and defensible.
- The copy is easy to understand.
- The page avoids hype.
- Sustainability is connected to business value.
- Security and privacy are mentioned where relevant.
- The page supports T1, T2, or T3 strategy.
- The page supports EU or LATAM positioning when relevant.
- The page uses approved colors and typography.
- The copy is modular enough for web components.
