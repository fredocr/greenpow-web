# Campaign Architecture

## Campaign Name

Powered by Purpose.

## Campaign Concept

A storytelling series showing how different teams use GREENPOW to grow responsibly.

## T1 Campaign

Theme: Green scaling stories.

Message: Start small. Build responsibly. Grow on carbon-aware infrastructure.

Formats:

- Founder interviews.
- Short videos.
- Dev.to posts.
- Social clips.
- Startup launch guides.

## T2 Campaign

Theme: ESG alignment journeys.

Message: Scale your platform while improving sustainability visibility and infrastructure control.

Formats:

- Webinars.
- Case studies.
- ROI calculators.
- Cloud migration guides.
- SaaS infrastructure playbooks.

## T3 Campaign

Theme: Scope 3 and digital infrastructure enablement.

Message: Modernize critical infrastructure with secure, traceable, carbon-aware cloud.

Formats:

- Executive briefs.
- White papers.
- RFP kits.
- ESG documentation.
- Board-level decks.

## Europe Campaign

Title: Cloud for Net-Zero Digital Operations.

Audience: T2 and T3 EU.

Message: Build scalable cloud infrastructure that supports ESG goals, data protection, and carbon visibility.

Channels:

- LinkedIn.
- WebSummit.
- Berlin tech events.
- Sustainability conferences.
- ESG newsletters.
- CTO and CIO communities.

CTA: Download the EU carbon-aware cloud brief.

## LATAM Campaign

Title: TI verde sin comprometer costos.

Audience: T1, T2, and T3 LATAM.

Message: Infraestructura cloud segura, eficiente y sostenible para equipos que quieren crecer con confianza.

Channels:

- LinkedIn LATAM.
- Regional webinars.
- ANDICOM.
- Fintech communities.
- University partnerships.
- Partner-led workshops.

CTA: Agenda una consulta regional.
