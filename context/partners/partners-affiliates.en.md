# Partners And Affiliates

## Partner Narrative

GREENPOW partners with organizations that can help distribute, integrate, host, operate, or expand carbon-aware infrastructure.

## Partner Types

- Cloud providers.
- Data centers.
- Hosting companies.
- VPS providers.
- Managed service providers.
- Sustainability consultants.
- ESG software providers.
- AI workflow and automation companies.
- Regional infrastructure and sovereign cloud partners.

## Partner Value

Partners can expand their offering with carbon-aware infrastructure, Scope 2 visibility, cloud optimization, and AI infrastructure positioning.
