# Press And Media

## Press Positioning

GREENPOW is a carbon-aware cloud infrastructure company reducing the carbon footprint of cloud computing by optimizing where and when compute runs across public cloud, private cloud, VPS, hosting, and distributed infrastructure.

## Short Description

GREENPOW helps organizations reduce cloud emissions by routing workloads using real-time carbon, energy, cost, and infrastructure signals.

## Boilerplate

GREENPOW builds carbon-aware cloud infrastructure technology for the AI and cloud era. The company helps organizations make better infrastructure decisions by combining carbon intensity, energy price, cost, performance, and infrastructure availability signals. GREENPOW supports public cloud optimization, sustainable VPS and hosting, private cloud, AI agents infrastructure, and Cortex, an organizational memory layer for autonomous agents.

## Media Narrative

Cloud infrastructure decisions are often static, while energy prices, grid carbon intensity, cost, performance, and infrastructure availability change continuously. GREENPOW focuses on turning those dynamic signals into practical infrastructure decisions.

The company should be described as product-led infrastructure technology, not only as a hosting provider or managed services company.

- Carbon-aware cloud infrastructure.
- Cleaner compute.
- Carbon-aware workload routing.
- Cloud infrastructure optimization.
- Scope 2 visibility.
- AI agents infrastructure.
- Organizational memory for autonomous agents.

## Terms To Avoid

- Zero-emission cloud.
- Carbon neutral by default.
- Guaranteed carbon reduction for every workload.
- Generic eco-hosting as the primary description.
- Claims that imply legal, regulatory, or environmental certification unless verified.

## Claim Discipline

Press copy must use precise and supportable language. Do not publish carbon reduction percentages, certification claims, customer names, investor names, partner names, or regulatory claims unless they are confirmed in approved source material.
