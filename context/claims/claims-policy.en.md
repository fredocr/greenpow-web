# Claims Policy

## Approved Claim Style

Use:

- Designed to reduce cloud-related emissions.
- Built for carbon-aware operations.
- Supports ESG visibility.
- Helps teams understand cloud carbon impact.
- Enables more sustainable infrastructure decisions.
- Provides lower-emission cloud options.
- Supports privacy-first cloud operations.
- Lower-emission.
- Carbon-aware.
- Designed to reduce.
- Measurable impact.
- Built for ESG visibility.
- Supports sustainability reporting.
- Helps reduce infrastructure-related emissions.

## Avoid Without Proof

Do not use:

- Zero emissions.
- Zero-impact cloud.
- 100% carbon neutral.
- Guaranteed carbon neutral.
- Completely emission-free.
- The only green cloud.
- The greenest cloud in the world.
- Guaranteed carbon elimination.
- Guaranteed carbon reduction.
- Fully compliant with every regulation.
- Certified for everything.
- Automatically makes your company compliant.
- Solves Scope 3 completely.
- No environmental impact.
- Instant ESG compliance.

## When Making Environmental Claims

Always be clear about:

- What is being measured.
- How it is measured.
- What is reduced versus compensated.
- What assumptions apply.
- What the customer can verify.

## Investment Evidence Labels

Investor-facing claims and metrics must use one of these labels:

- Achieved.
- Observed.
- Measured.
- Modeled.
- Projected.
- Target.
- Benchmark.
- R&D validated.
- Investor estimate.

Do not present projections, modeled upside, investor benchmarks, or targets as achieved facts.

## Investor Carbon Claim Language

Use:

- Observed in production workloads.
- Varies by workload and region.
- Demonstrated in controlled R&D environments.
- Modeled reduction potential.
- Confidence-labeled climate claims.

Avoid:

- Guaranteed emissions reduction.
- Zero carbon cloud.
- Carbon neutral by default.
- Eliminates emissions.
- Verified globally unless third-party verification exists.

## Proof Categories

### Technical Proof

- Architecture diagrams.
- Uptime metrics.
- Security standards.
- Platform documentation.
- API or integration docs.
- Case studies.

### Sustainability Proof

- Carbon metrics.
- Renewable energy logic.
- Emissions tracking methodology.
- Carbon-aware workload examples.
- Impact dashboards.
- Compensation and reduction methodology.

### Business Proof

- Customer stories.
- Cost efficiency.
- ROI examples.
- Reduced operational overhead.
- Faster deployment.
- Better reporting visibility.

### Regional Proof

- Berlin presence.
- LATAM support.
- Local partnerships.
- Academic partnerships.
- Regional events.
- Local language support.
